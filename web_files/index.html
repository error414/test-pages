Sentinel Automatic Antenna Tracker

Introduction

The AAT brings higher quality video and the ability to use lower video transmission power or increase the maximum reception distance over traditional omnidirectional antennas. The AAT provides the benefit of maintaining a more accurate direction over manually mounted antennas without the concern of the aircraft moving outside of the antenna’s dominant reception area.

Specifications and Requirements
